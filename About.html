Loteng Cafe Kuantan

ABOUT LOTENG CAFE

LoTeng Cafe Kuantan’s event space in Teluk Cempedak gets its unique name from the way the event space is structurally built as the word ‘loteng’ in the Malay language means attic or loft.Located two stories high, the event space claims to have the most breath-taking seafront view in Kuantan from its upper floor.The event space serves a good mix of Western and Thai cuisine and one should give the Spaghetti Beef Bolognese and Spicy TomYum a try.Dark Chocolate Toblerone and Salted Caramel cakes are truly great for dessert and go well with its ice-mixed drinks and Thai Milk tea. The plastic drinking tumblers that the drinks come in make a perfect keepsake too and their service also prompt and polite. Walked up the stairs to LoTeng Cafe Kuantan as they serve affordable Western-Thai cuisine and be warmly welcome by cheerfulness, natural lighting and lots of colour. The narrow event space located on the beach front has glass windows all around to let in the delightful cool sea breeze. The top floor is decorated with warm LED bulbs making it a truly romantic ambiance at night accompanied by the picturesque view of the setting sun overlooking the entire Teluk Cempedak beach. Furnishings and decorations are simple and come in many shades giving the event space a playful aura and a wonderful relaxed ambiance. Loteng also provide an ideal place to hold a birthday party, anniversary, proposal, farewell, reunion, or any family and friend gathering as the event space can hold up to 40 pax upstairs and another 40 downstairs.
